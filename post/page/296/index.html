Why are dogwoods called dogwoods?

published on August 02, 2024
Flowering dogwood is named for the showy spring flowers. The common name dogwood comes from one colonial description of the fruit as being edible but not fit for a dog. The common name dogwood is also thought to be from the use of the wood for skewers or “dogs.” Other common names include boxwood and cornel. What is the legend of the dogwood tree?
