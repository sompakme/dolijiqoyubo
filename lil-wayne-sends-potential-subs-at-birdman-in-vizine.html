Lil Wayne Sends Potential Subs At Birdman In &quot;Vizine&quot;

It's a great time to be a Lil Wayne fan. With two excellent Dedication mixtapes still earning rotation, Weezy continues his fire and fury with a new single called "Vizine." The track stems from lifestyle brand Ethika Inc's upcoming RGB mixtape, and finds the GOAT contender living up to his reputation; it would appear that Wayne's lyricism has only sharpened with age, and the punchlines that emerge from his mouth continue to defy expectations. If there's ever been a rapper that can make you stop and marvel at the sheer cleverness of it all, it's Weezy. Such is his way, and "Vizine" is no exception.

On the heels of reports that signify a new development in his ongoing legal battle with Birdman, "Vizine" finds Wayne throwing pointed subliminals at his former mentor. "No one man should have all that power if he can't afford to pay the light bills," raps Weezy, showing no remorse for Baby's recently foreclosed mansion. With rumors that Carter 5 may be dropping this year, it's safe to say that 2018 is quickly becoming the year of Wayne.
