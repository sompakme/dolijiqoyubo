John Entwistle Net Worth

John Entwistle was a legendary bassist born in London, England on October 9, 1944. He is best known for his bass line in the classic song "My Generation" by The Who. His immense talent was recognized when he was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1990.

About

Bassist for The Who whose bass line in "My Generation" is unforgettable. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

Trivia

Family Life

He married Alison Wise on June 23, 1967 and, after their divorce, he married Maxene Harlow on September 21, 1991. He has a son named Christopher.
