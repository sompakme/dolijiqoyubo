Laura Ingraham Plastic Surgery, What Happened To Laura Ingraham Face?

Spread the love

Laura Ingraham, a well-known American TV host, has been the subject of speculation regarding plastic surgery, particularly a facelift. Despite Ingraham denying these claims, internet users have noted changes in her appearance, especially in her jawline and cheekbones, which appear more contoured and prominent. Speculations suggest she might have undergone a Rhytidectomy (facelift) and possibly Botox injections.

Born on July 19, 1963, in Connecticut, USA, Laura Ingraham is currently 58 years old. She graduated from Glastonbury High School in 1981, earned her B.A. degree at Dartmouth College, and completed her Juris Doctor degree in 1991 from the University of Virginia’s School of Law. Ingraham, a breast cancer survivor, is also a mother to three adopted children.

Ingraham hosts “The Ingraham Angle” on Fox News Channel, airing on weeknights at 10 PM ET. The show, which debuted on October 30, 2017, is a conservative news and opinion program. Despite controversies and advertiser boycotts, it remains one of the top-rated shows in its time slot.

Regarding her personal life, Laura Ingraham has never been married.
