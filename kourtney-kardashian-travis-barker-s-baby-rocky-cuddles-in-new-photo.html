Kourtney Kardashian, Travis Barkers Baby Rocky Cuddles in New Photo

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker’s baby Rocky Thirteen is getting so big! The 7-month-old looked so chunky and cute sitting on his mom’s lap in a new photo, and he is the cutest little cuddler.

Barker’s daughter Alabama, 18, who he shares with ex Shanna Moakler, shared the picture of her baby brother on her Instagram story on Monday. “My favorite people 🫶🏻” she captioned the photo.

Barker and Kardashian sit side by side on what looks to be the Blink-182 tour bus. The drummer wears a skull hoodie and holds a blanket on his lap, and the Lemme founder sits next to him in a black hoodie with fire on the sleeves. Their baby is in white pajamas as he leans into give his mama the biggest hug. His legs are wrapped around her waist and his head is resting on her shoulder. You can’t see much of the big boy, as his face is turned toward the window and out of the shot, but you can see his dark brown hair just like his mama’s. He’s so adorable! This kid’s fashion is next-level!

Happy to celebrate you!”
