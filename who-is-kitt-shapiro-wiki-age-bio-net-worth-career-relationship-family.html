Who is Kitt Shapiro? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship, Family

Kitt Shapiro, an American businesswoman, has made her mark as a successful entrepreneur and the owner of Simply Eartha LLC. Apart from her professional achievements, let’s explore her personal life, including her husband, children, parents, and net worth.

Husband and Marriage

  • Kitt Shapiro is happily married to Allan Bruce Rothschild, the son of Rita and Stanley Rothschild.
  • The couple tied the knot in 2002 and have been inseparable ever since.

Children

  • Kitt Shapiro and Allan Bruce Rothschild are proud parents to two children, Rachel Shapiro and Jason Shapiro.
  • Their family bond is strong, and they cherish every moment spent together.

Parentage and Family Background

  • Born on November 26, 1961, in Los Angeles, California, Kitt Shapiro is the daughter of John William McDonald and the legendary Eartha Kitt.
  • Eartha Kitt, her mother, was an acclaimed American singer, actress, comedian, dancer, and activist, known for her unique singing style.
  • Unfortunately, Eartha Kitt passed away on December 25, 2008, in Weston, Connecticut, leaving behind a remarkable legacy.

Impressive Net Worth
