Malachiae Warren Feat. Migos &quot;Thank Yo Momma&quot; Video

Malachiae Warren is a young singer/songwriter from Atlanta, whose new single, "Thank Yo Momma," features Migos, or, due to Offset's current incarceration, Quavo and Takeoff. Warren has just given the smooth club track a new video, which also features the two bando bros. 

The video starts with Warren attending a female-infested pool party hosted by Atlanta rapper Kap G. He then travels to the Cascade roller rink in Atlanta, where he mobs out with Quavo, who showcases his auto-tuned R&B skills, and Takeoff, who drops a hard verse. The track is, of course, an ode to the all the mothers out there for the beautiful daughters they have bestowed upon the ATL community.
