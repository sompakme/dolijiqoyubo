JAMB: Man Who Wrote UTME 6 Times Goes to University, Becomes a Lawyer, Celebrates Call to Bar

  • A man has shared the number of times he wrote JAMB UTME before he was able to gain admission to study at the university
  • The man, Uthman Isa Tochukwu, who is now a lawyer, said he wrote the exam six times years ago before he made it
  • Uthman made a post on social media to share his JAMB experience and to celebrate his call to the bar as a lawyer

A Nigerian man revealed the number of times he sat for the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) examinations before he was able to make it to a higher institution.

Uthman Isa Tochukwu took to X to share that he sat for the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) six times before he became a university student.

Uthman made a throwback post to celebrate after he successfully went to school and became a lawyer.
