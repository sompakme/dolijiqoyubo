OutDaughtered's Danielle Busby Reveals The Truth About Plastic Surgery

On Jan. 7, 2021, Danielle Busby gave fans the update they were hoping for when she took to Instagram to reveal that she would not need to go under the knife. "I went into my procedure on Monday pretty certain that I would be coming out with having to have a surgery, but my prayer was answered and NO surgery is needed," she enthused, continuing, "I am thanking YOU ALL for the extra prayers because I believe in the POWER of PRAYER and I am certain these prayers helped my outcome."

Even so, the reality TV star clarified that the positive result "doesn't mean this is the end to my current struggles. More test, lead to more doctors... but still no clear answer at the moment of what's going on," she wrote before asking for privacy once more. "Please understand, I am in the window of uncertainty and I only feel comfortable sharing as much as this," she added.

The mystery illness, however, isn't the only reason that Danielle's medical history has been the subject of scrutiny. In January 2021, fans speculated that Danielle had undergone cosmetic surgery.
