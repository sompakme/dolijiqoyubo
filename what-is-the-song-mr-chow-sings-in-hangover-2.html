What is a hangover in its truest sense?

A hangover is the sensation of many unpleasant physiological and psychological symptoms that occur after consuming alcoholic beverages such as wine, beer, or distilled spirits, such as hard liquor. Following a night of excessive alcohol consumption, the symptoms usually manifest themselves when the intoxicating impact of alcohol starts to wear off, usually the following morning.
