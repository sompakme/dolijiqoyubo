Stream Monica's New Album &quot;Code Red&quot;

After a 3-year hiatus, R&B singer Monica returns with her 8th studio album called Code Red.

Led by the Lil Wayne-featured hit “Just Right For Me,” the follow up to 2012’s New Life comes with 14 tracks in total and features guest appearances from Akon, Missy Elliott, Timbaland, Lil Wayne, and more. Available on iTunes, fans can now stream the album for free thanks to the good folks at Spotify.

Take a listen and let us know what track you’re feelin’ the most.
