Ravesi's is situated on Campbell Parade, the main strip of Bondi Beach, and is as close to the sand as it could possibly get. It’s one of the best spots in the suburb, occupying the prized corner of Hall Street and Campbell Parade in the epicentre of the locale’s dining and shopping district and footsteps to the surf. Paying homage to the Art Deco heritage of the building, the famous arched windows have been painted flamingo pink, as has the interior of the wrap around terrace.
