Who is Ben Seewald? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

  • Ben Seewald is an American reality television personality, football coach, handyman, and Instagram star from Hot Springs, Arkansas.
  • He is best known as the spouse of Jessa Duggar, an American TV star.
  • Ben has appeared in the reality show “19 Kids and Counting” and its spin-off “Counting On” with his family.

Early Life and Family:

Career and Television:

  • Ben Seewald began his television career by appearing in the last season of “19 Kids and Counting,” where the focus was on his relationship with Jessa Duggar.

Relationship and Marriage:
