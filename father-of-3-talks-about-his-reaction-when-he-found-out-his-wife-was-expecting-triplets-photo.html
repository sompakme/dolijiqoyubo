Father of 3 talks about his reaction when he found out his wife was expecting triplets (photo)

- A man and his wife who have three children are expecting triplets

- The man shared the story of how his wife shared the news with him

- He also noted that he went to sleep immediately he realized that he was expecting triplets

A couple, Robert and Nia Tolbert, who have three beautiful boys are expecting triplets in the United States.

Robert who shared the story with ABC News expressed that his wife broke the news to him by leaving a gift bag for him with a handwritten note.

According to the expecting father, he went to bed to sleep immediately he understood what the gift bag meant.

He said: “I opened the bag and I saw a very, very long sonogram, then I saw three onesies in the bag… and they were numbered one, two, and three.”

“When I finally understood, I was just shocked and I just went straight to bed.”

His wife, Nia expressed that they have both recovered from the shock and they are preparing for the future.

She said: “We’re already planning for our new household. It’s going to be equally balanced now with three little girls and three little boys.”

According to her, they realised things are not going to be easy from the moment on but they are ready for it.

Legit.ng gathered that triplet births are rare in the United States.
