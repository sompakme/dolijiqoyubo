Photos That Nearly Broke The Internet

In 2013, Brazilian supermodel Gisele Bundchen posted the ultimate multitasking photo to her Instagram feed. In the picture, Bundchen is breastfeeding her 1-year-old daughter while getting a manicure, her hair styled and makeup applied by three assistants. Naturally, the internet went slightly crazy over the photo, with some criticizing Bundchen for making public what they felt should be a private moment. As Denise Albert, co-creator of TheMoms.com told ABC News, "I think breastfeeding is a very personal thing. And for her to put this on Instagram while she's getting her hair and makeup done is a little outrageous, and I think obnoxious."

Other fans jumped to Bundchen's defense, praising her for promoting breastfeeding. The model has long been against bottle feeding, once tellling Harper's Bazaar, "I think there should be a worldwide law, in my opinion, that mothers should breastfeed their babies for six months."
