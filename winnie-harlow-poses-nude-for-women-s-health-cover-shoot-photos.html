Winnie Harlow Poses Nude For &quot;Women's Health&quot; Cover Shoot: Photos

Last month, Winnie Harlow made headlines as the face of Drake's OVO campaign. In April, the Canadian starlet continues to expand her undeniably impressive modelling resume, this time stripping down for the cover of Women's Health. For the magazine's May issue, she not only posed nude for the cover and various other photos, but Harlow also got candid about the rejection she's faced within the industry as a result of her skin condition. Because of her unique look, the socialite has made it something of a personal mission for herself to "change the definition of beauty."

"I got a lot of no's along the way, but I just pushed through," the 28-year-old told the outlet while reflecting on how vitiligo has impacted her career. "My younger self always believed in the power of faith, strength, and perseverance, while learning vulnerability and being authentically myself," Harlow added. "To achieve my goals with the world watching has been scary at times, but all in all, the ultimate blessing." As DailyMail notes, she first caught the public's eye in 2014 while competing on the 21st cycle of America's Next Top Model.
