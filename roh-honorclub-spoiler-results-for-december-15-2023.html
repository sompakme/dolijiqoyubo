ROH HonorClub Spoiler Results for December 15, 2023

Ring of Honor recently recorded a set of television episodes at the Collision event held at the Bell Centre in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. The event featured several notable matches, and here are the results that will air on December 15, 2023. Here are the results sent in from our correspondent at the event:

Leyla Hirsch defeated Katrina Krize.

In a Pure Rules Match, ROH Pure Champion Wheeler Yuta defeated Jason Geiger.

Taya Valkyrie (accompanied by Johnny TV) defeated Rachael Ellering (with Leyla Hirsch and Maria Kanellis).

Angelico defeated Christopher Daniels. Following the match, Angelico and Serpentico attacked Daniels, but Orange Cassidy and Danhausen came to his rescue.
