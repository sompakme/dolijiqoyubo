Celebrity Birthdays 26th April: Channing Tatum, Melania Trump and Jordana Brewster

What do Hollywood heartthrob Channing Tatum, US First Lady Melania Trump and actress Jordana Brewster all have in common? It's their birthday today! To celebrate their special days, we're taking a look at what these three famous faces are up to at the moment.

Channing Tatum turns 38

Happy birthday to Magic Mike star Channing Tatum! The hunky actor started celebrations early when he partied at the Honda Indy Grand Prix in Birmingham, Alabama last week. He has had a tough time recently, after announcing that he and his wife of almost nine years, Jenna Dewan Tatum, had "lovingly chosen to separate as a couple." The split was amicable; the couple said in their joint statement that they are "two best friends realising its time to take some space and help each other live the most joyous lives possible". However, a source close to the couple recently told People magazine that the breakup has been "hard for Channing," describing it as a "big adjustment for him".

Melania Trump turns 48

Happy birthday to the US First Lady, Melania Trump! It has been a busy week leading up to her birthday. Melania has attended the funeral of former First Lady Barbara Bush, before preparing to host a state visit from French President Emmanuel Macron. This year will mark Melania's first birthday spent in the White House!

Jordana Brewster turns 38

Happy birthday to Jordana Brewster! Not only does the Fast and Furious star share her birthday with Channing Tatum, the two are exactly the same age! Jordana recently attended the New York premiere of horror film A Quiet Place, which was produced by her husband Andrew Form. Jordana will most likely be spending her birthday surrounded by her loved ones, including her two children, Julian, four, and one-year-old Rowan.
