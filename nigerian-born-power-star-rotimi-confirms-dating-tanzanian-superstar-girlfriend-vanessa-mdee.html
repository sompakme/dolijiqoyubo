Nigerian-born Power star Rotimi confirms dating Tanzanian superstar girlfriend Vanessa Mdee

Nigerian-born American series actor and singer Olurotimi Akinosho, who is popularly known as Andre Coleman in much-watched American TV series, Power, has finally given an answer to the trending rumour of his relationship with Tanzanian born singer, Vanessa Mdee.

On Monday, November 25, the Tanzanian singer took to her Instagram page to share a loved-up photo of herself and Rotimi while they posed inside an elevator during a date night. She captioned it: “Monday Night is Date Night.”

Rotimi on his own Instagram page also shared their loved up photo and for him, it was not a time for a long story but a short yet strong affirmative three-letter word of what the two of them share. He captioned the photo: “HER.”

This post is coming days after the Tanzanian singer confirmed that she is in a relationship with the Power star during an interview on Clouds FM.
