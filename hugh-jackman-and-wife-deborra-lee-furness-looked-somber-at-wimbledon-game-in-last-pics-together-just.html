Hugh Jackman and wife Deborra-lee Furness looked somber at Wimbledon game in last pics together just

HUGH Jackman and his wife, Deborra-lee Furness looked down and somber weeks before their split, final photos of the couple together have revealed.

The couple announced on Friday that they are separating after 27 years of marriage as they plan to focus on "individual growth."

The last time the couple was photographed together was at this year's Wimbledon competition in London, England.

On July 16, 2023, Hugh, 54, and Deborra-lee, 67, attended the men's final on Centre Court between Carlos Alcaraz vs Novak Djokovic.

The couple also sat with Djokovic's wife, Jelena.

The Hollywood actor and his wife sat next to each other without a smile on their faces.

THEIR NEXT CHAPTER

On September 15, the couple made a statement to People about their relationship status.

Hugh and Deborra-lee said: "We have been blessed to share almost 3 decades together as husband and wife in a wonderful, loving marriage. Our journey now is shifting and we have decided to separate to pursue our individual growth,"

The statement continued: "Our family has been and always will be our highest priority. We undertake this next chapter with gratitude, love, and kindness.

"We greatly appreciate your understanding in respecting our privacy as our family navigates this transition in all of our lives."

"This is the sole statement either of us will make," the pair concluded.

THEIR RELATIONSHIP

The couple met on the set of the Australian TV show Correlli in 1995 and married a year later.

“Deb, she was a big star…I get picked up, and Deb is in the front seat of the car. I’ll never forget,” Hugh told People back in 2017.

He continued to describe how they met.

“She took off her seatbelt and she turned around and put out her hand and took off her sunglasses and said, ‘Hi, I’m Deborra-lee Furness, nice to meet you.'"

"I remember thinking, ‘I like this girl.'"

Hugh and Deborra-lee have two children: son Oscar, 23, and daughter Ava, 18.

MORE ON HUGH JACKMAN

Hugh Michael Jackman was born on October 12, 1968, in Sydney, Australia.

He has an impressive movie catalog including The Greatest Showman, Logan, Wolverine, X-Men, Les Misérables, and more.

His upcoming films include Deadpool 3 and The Amateur.
