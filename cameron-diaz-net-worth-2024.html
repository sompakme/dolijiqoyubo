Early Life and Career Beginnings

Cameron Diaz was born in San Diego, California, and began her career as a model at the age of 16. She worked with big brands like Calvin Klein and Levi’s. However, her life took a turn when she auditioned for “The Mask” and landed the female lead role, marking her entry into Hollywood.

Hollywood Success

After her successful debut, Diaz went on to star in numerous successful films. Her performances in “My Best Friend’s Wedding” and “There’s Something About Mary” earned her Golden Globe nominations. Her role in “Being John Malkovich” further solidified her position in Hollywood.

Charlie’s Angels and Shrek

Diaz’s career reached new heights when she starred in “Charlie’s Angels” alongside Drew Barrymore and Lucy Liu. The film was a box office hit, and Diaz reportedly earned $12 million for her role. She also lent her voice to the character of Princess Fiona in the “Shrek” series, which was a massive success.

Other Ventures

Besides acting, Diaz has also ventured into other areas. She co-authored two health books: “The Body Book” and “The Longevity Book,” which became New York Times bestsellers. She also ventured into the wine business with her brand “Avaline.”

Endorsements and Investments

Diaz has endorsed several brands throughout her career, including Tag Heuer, Softbank, and Pour La Victoire. These endorsements have significantly contributed to her net worth. She has also made smart investments in real estate, owning properties in Los Angeles and New York.

Retirement from Acting

In 2014, Diaz announced her retirement from acting to focus on her personal life. Despite not appearing in films since then, her net worth continues to grow thanks to her various business ventures and investments.

Impact on Net Worth

While her retirement might have halted her income from films, Diaz’s net worth hasn’t taken a hit. Her successful ventures outside of acting have ensured a steady increase in her wealth.

Philanthropy

Diaz is also known for her philanthropic efforts. She supports numerous charities, including UNICEF, the Environmental Media Association, and the Innocence Project. While these efforts might not directly contribute to her net worth, they certainly add to her overall value as a public figure.

Environmental Activism

As an environmental activist, Diaz has used her platform to raise awareness about environmental issues. She has participated in various campaigns and documentaries, further enhancing her public image and indirectly contributing to her net worth.

Personal Life

Diaz is married to musician Benji Madden, and they have a daughter together. Her personal life has remained relatively private, which has helped her maintain a positive public image.
