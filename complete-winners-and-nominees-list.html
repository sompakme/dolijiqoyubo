Complete Winners And Nominees List

In the wake of Whitney Houston‘s tragic death, one day before the music industry’s most important night, the stars descended on L.A.’s Staples Center for the 54th annual Grammy Awards. 

Kanye West led the nominations with seven mentions including song of the year and best rap-sung collaboration. 

PHOTOS: Grammy Awards 2012 Red Carpet Arrivals

Vying for the top award, album of the year, will be Adele (21), Foo Fighters (Wasting Light), Lady Gaga (Born This Way), Bruno Mars (Doo-Wops & Hooligans) and Rihanna (Loud).

Adele and Mars also will compete for record of the year honors with “Rolling in the Deep” and “Grenade,” respectively. Also nominated in that category were Bon Iver (“Holocene”), Mumford & Sons (“The Cave”) and Katy Perry (“Firework”).
