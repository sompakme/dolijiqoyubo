Best Discounts, Deals for The Iconic Right Now

THE ICONIC has got to be one of Australia’s favourite shopping destinations thanks to its speedy shipping, generous return policy and — of course — fantastic sales that run all year around.

To make things easier for you we’ve rounded up the best of the best sales and discounts at THE ICONIC right now and will be updating this page frequently as more deals come through.

The retailer is holding their end of season sale with up to 60 per cent off. Plus, they’ve also gone early with their Boxing Day sales, offering 30 per cent off a huge range of prices.

As always please remember that prices and deals are current at the time of publishing but items can move quickly during sales, so be quick if you don’t want to miss out.

Our team of experienced writers spend hours researching products to include in articles. We find out what factors make a good product by talking to experts, reviewing test results and analysing thousands of customer reviews.

THE ICONIC offers free returns for change of mind 30 days from the date of purchase (exclusions to some items apply for hygiene reasons, find out more here), however, sale items marked “final sale” or “non-returnable” will be exempt from this policy.

To complete a return items must be unworn, unused and still in its original packaging — find out more here.

To return an item, log into your THE ICONIC account and select “returns”, then follow the steps to get your prepaid return label. You can reuse the satchel your items came in, simply fix your returns label over the top of the original sender label and seal it back up tightly!

For specific info on what shipping to your area is like plus costs you can click here. For metro areas THE ICONIC offers same-day and same-evening shipping options (prices and times vary according to city). For metro standard delivery is free for orders over $75 or $8.95 for orders under that amount. For regional/country areas there are express shipping options available, however, again this will vary from state-to-state.
