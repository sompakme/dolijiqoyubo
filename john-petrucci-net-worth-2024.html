Early Life and Career Beginnings

John Petrucci was born on July 12, 1967, in Kings Park, New York. He developed an interest in music at a young age and started playing the guitar when he was 12. His passion for music led him to attend the Berklee College of Music in Boston, where he met future bandmates John Myung and Mike Portnoy. Together, they formed the band Majesty, which later became Dream Theater.

Dream Theater and Rise to Fame

Dream Theater, formed in 1985, has been a significant contributor to Petrucci’s net worth. The band has released 14 studio albums, with several achieving gold and platinum status. Petrucci’s intricate guitar work and songwriting skills have been instrumental in the band’s success.
