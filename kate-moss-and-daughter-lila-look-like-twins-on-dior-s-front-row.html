Kate Moss and Daughter Lila Look Like Twins on Dior's Front Row

Kate Moss has arrived at Paris Fashion Week, and she brought daughter Lila Moss as her stylish date.

The mother-daughter duo were spotted sitting front row at Dior’s Menswear Fall/Winter 2024–25 runway show today, and looked incredible in sleek, coordinating looks. Kate wore a light blue sleeveless top embellished with matching beads all over, plus black trousers and black leather over-the-knee stiletto boots. She accessorized with diamond hoops, a white pearl cuff, a gold bangle, and a diamond-encrusted watch. As for glam, the iconic ’90s supermodel wore her hair in casual waves and had on a subtle smoky eye.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Next to her, look-alike daughter Lila looked so chic in a boxy gray wool button-up jacket, which she layered over a black top and wore with black straight-leg pants and matching leather boots with block heels. She wore minimal makeup and had her hair straight down.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

The 21-year-old has been following in her mom’s footsteps since her early teens. When she turned 18, she made her catwalk debut at Miu Miu’s October 2020 show in Paris. (Kate also walked in her first runway show when she was 18, appearing on the catwalk for Dolce & Gabbana in 1992.) Since then, Lila has landed various fashion and beauty campaigns—Marc Jacobs Beauty, Maje’s Fall 2023 collection, Calvin Klein Jewelry—and magazine covers, including for Vogue Italia (where she appeared with her mom), Vanity Fair Italia, and British Vogue (which she also appeared in with Kate).
