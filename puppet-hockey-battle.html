Puppet Hockey Battle

Game Description

Let’s play hockey! In this fun sports game, it’s the ultimate puppet battle. Choose your favorite country as your team and get ready to skate, jump, and shoot goals against your opponent in this fun battle!

Game Instructions

Tap or click the arrow to play. Select your team. To play the game, use your LEFT and RIGHT arrows to move back and forth. Use the UP arrow to jump. Press “F” to move your hockey stick and score. Race against the clock and WIN!

Good luck!
