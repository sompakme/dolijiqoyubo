Gizem Erdogan Fan Casting

Gizem Suna Kling Erdogan, born April 30, 1987 in Högsbo, is a Swedish actor.

She studied at the theater school in Malmö in 2012-2015. She has participated in performances at the Kulturhuset Stadsteatern in Stockholm and is part of the Göteborg Stadsteater's permanent ensemble, where she has played Alice in the play Alice in Wonderland, among other things. In 2015, she made her feature film debut in "Svenskjävel".
