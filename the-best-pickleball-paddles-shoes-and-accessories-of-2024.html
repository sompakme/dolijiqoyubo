The Best Pickleball Paddles, Shoes and Accessories of 2024

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, The Hollywood Reporter may receive an affiliate commission.

Pickleball continues to explode in popularity, creating a new niche in fashion offerings from luxe labels and sportswear brands alike. Everyone from Selena Gomez to Ellen DeGeneres is getting in on the game, while Michael B. Jordan, Drake, Heidi Klum, Eva Longoria, Chris Evert and Michael Phelps are among the star investors in Major League Pickleball teams.

On March 4 and 5, Charlize Theron will host the 20th annual Desert Smash charity celebrity tennis tournament, which will include its first-ever pickleball challenge. Last September, the inaugural Pickle! for Purpose Celebrity Pickleball Invitational took over Pepperdine University in Malibu, where stars such as James Marsden and Sophia Bush attended in support of the Targeting Emotional and Mental Stability (TEAMS) Act.

The new racket-ready looks — chic enough to move from court to cafe — include swingy skirts and dresses, stylish tank tops, paddles and sneakers with punches of color, even limited-edition paddles and a personalized tote bag to stash all the gear. A multitude of matchy-matchy sets hit that fashion-meets-function sweet spot, making it simple to look pulled together on the pickleball court and beyond.

Ahead, shop some of the best pickleball paddles, accessories, shoes and clothing for spring.

On Holiday Pickleball Set

Get the game going with San Diego-based brand On Holiday Pickleball’s starter bundles that include your choice of one to eight fun graphic-patterned paddles, three pickleball (in white or Dayglo colors) and a cotton mesh Tour Court Pickleball Bag that pulls all the gear together.

Aviator Nation Colorblock Bralette Sports Bra

In five colorways, the Aviator Nation Color Block Bralette Sports Bra, with a racer back, raises the bra game in style. Plus the comfy two-way stretch fabric, crafted from recycled yarn, has built-in UV protection. Designed to coordinate with matching biker shorts and leggings for an easy set win.

Tory Sport Matching Ripstop Anorak & Shorts Set

The Tory Sport offshoot of Hollywood-loved brand Tory Burch has a wide array of adorable, coordinating pickleball garb. We love these Ripstop Gradient shorts that are lightweight, with  an easygoing elastic waist, plenty of pockets, and a cool wash of color.  Look polished going to and from the court by adding the coordinating anorak, with more pockets and adjustable bungee cords. The pieces are also an extra 10% off at checkout.

Lilly Pulitzer UPF 50+ Luxletic Ace Active Dress and Bag

In Barbie pink with a “ready to resort” vibe, this darling long-sleeved Lilly Pulitzer Luxletic Ace Active dress is crafted from partially recycled fabric with UPF 50+ sun protection. The moisture-wicking, racerback silhouette has a built-in shelf bra and undershorts with a handy pocket. Bonus: A matching belt bag to keep valuables close, hands-free.

Beyond Yoga Spacedye Essence Dress

Beyond Yoga’s Spacedye Essence dress has a built-in bodysuit and adjustable spaghetti straps — so you’ll feel comfortable serving on the court and during post-game errands.

The Upside Form Seamless Angie Crop and 25-in. Midi LEggings

The sporty color-block effect of the contrasting seams on The Upside’s Form Seamless crop top and matching midi leggings subtly channel superhero style. The high-waist cut elongates legs and flatters the tummy, while the super-soft fabric has four-way stretch for ultimate comfort. 

Head Revolt Evo 2.0 Women Court Shoes

Put your best foot forward on the court and beyond in Head’s Revolt Evo 2.0 court sneakers with an extra-wide fit, a breathable and lightweight mesh upper, triple density midsole and a durable outsole designed for the best grip on clay and tennis surfaces.

Recess x Gray Malin Beach Print Paddle Set

The Los Angeles-based fine arts photographer Gray Malin has translated his signature aerial beach photography into a collaborative racket collection with pickleball brand Recess. Complete with three pickleballs and two limited-edition leather trim cases, this set takes the sweat out of planning; simply find a partner to play.

Wilson Vintage Tank Top

With cooler temps landing, the throwback look and added warmth of the Wilson Vintage Tank Top makes it a pivotal layering piece in any pickleball wardrobe. We love the comfortable cut and retro vibe, plus the tech cotton fabric is formulated to repel odors. Simply toss it over the top of a tee, tank, sports bra or dress.

Alo Yoga In the Lead Skirt

With an adjustable bungee-cinch waist, built-in short, and functional pockets, Alo Yoga’s In The Lead skirt is pretty practical in classic black. Give it a cool edge with the Cropped Prestige Polo top.

Mark & Graham Sporty Stripe Pickleball Bag

Whether you’re shopping for yourself or your favorite court-made, a personalized Mark & Graham Sporty Stripe Pickleball Bag keeps gear stylishly organized. The carryall smartly fits a racket and other essentials — it even has a water bottle pocket inside. Available in other colors; the brand offers many other customizable gifts for pickleball players, including paddles, waterproof totes and crossbodies.

Staud Rally Paddle Case

Frequently seen on stars, LA-based fashion brand Staud has added a Staud Court capsule line of some of the most covetable pickleball styles we discovered, from rompers and dresses to shoes and accessories. Our pick: The sleek butterscotch-toned leather Rally Paddle Case.
