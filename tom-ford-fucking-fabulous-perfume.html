Tom Ford Fucking Fabulous Perfume

In beauty news that surprises absolutely no one, Tom Ford is releasing a new fragrance simply called Fucking Fabulous. Yes, that is the real name.

Courtesy of Tom Ford

The limited edition fragrance is debuting at Ford's spring/summer 2018 runway show in New York on September 6th, and will be exclusively available the next day in Tom Ford boutiques and on TomFord.com. The key notes are almond bitter oil, tonka resinoid, orris accord, cashmeran, leather accord, and clary sage oil—which sounds like Fucking Fabulous will be another sexy, earthy, and bold scent from the master of such things.

All that bravado comes at a hefty price: $310 for 50ml and $495 for the leathery luxe atomizer. But you'll probably crack a smile when you look at it on your vanity everyday—so that's a small price to pay, right?
