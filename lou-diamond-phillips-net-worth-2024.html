Lou Diamond Phillips’ Early Life

Born on February 17, 1962, in Subic Bay Naval Station, Philippines, Lou Diamond Phillips moved to Texas with his family when he was still a child. He developed an interest in acting during his high school years and pursued this passion at the University of Texas at Arlington, where he graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Drama.
