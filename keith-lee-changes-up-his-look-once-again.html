Keith Lee Changes Up His Look Once Again

Keith Lee has gone through a few phases during his pro wrestling journey. The former NXT Champion has been spotted in several attires, and he was also known for his white hair, but that time is in the past.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Rampage from this week.

During AEW Rampage this week, The Hardyz and Keith Lee defeated Kip Sabian, The Butcher, and The Blade. That was a fun match, and many fans enjoyed themselves, but Keith Lee was rocking a different look.

Keith Lee’s white hair is now gone. He has dyed his hair once again. We’re not sure what caused this change, but he is a babyface now, so that might have been a catalyst for that alteration.

Keith Lee has never been ashamed about his white hair, which is his natural look. He claims that white hair has plagued him since high school, and he embraced it in a big way. Apparently, he decided to go a bit darker with his hair color now.

Keith Lee once said that creative freedom in AEW was big selling point for him. It seems that Tony Khan gave him the freedom to bring back his white hair and take it away as well.

We will have to see what is next for Keith Lee in AEW. He is a babyface, and winning matches on television. Only time will tell if he can finally reach the pinnacle of the company, as he did in NXT.
