Remaining WWE WrestleMania Tickets Going For Insane Prices

WWE is gearing up for a monumental two-night extravaganza with WrestleMania 40, and the response from fans has been nothing short of extraordinary. Tickets for the event went on sale today, and the reception was massive. The prices are through the roof too.

With WrestleMania spanning two nights, the available seating capacity for the show essentially doubled. However, the initial demand for this grand spectacle was absolutely remarkable. That will also mean doubt profit for WWE and everyone involved.

According to Wrestle Tix, 90,000 tickets for WWE WrestleMania 40 have already been sold, encompassing both nights of the event. In the process, they have shattered WWE’s all-time gate record.

Also, the current starting price for a two-day combo ticket on Ticketmaster for WWE WrestleMania 40 is approximately $3,000. That does not even include Ticketmaster’s additional fees, which can be quite steep.

This historic event in the world of sports entertainment marks a return to Philadelphia for the first time in 25 years as WrestleMania 40 descends upon Lincoln Financial Field. The action is set to unfold on April 6th and 7th, 2024. No matches have been announced for the show of shows, but the event is still an incredibly hot ticket.
