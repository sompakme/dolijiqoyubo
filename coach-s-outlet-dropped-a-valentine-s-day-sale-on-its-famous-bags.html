Mini Jamie Camera Bag In Signature Canvas With Heart Print

The Mini Jamie Camera Bag, one of the brand’s most iconic crossbody silhouettes, got a seriously cute update for Valentine’s Day. This version features white leather details, the brand’s signature monogrammed print in khaki, and a pale pink heart at its center — a festive detail that’s subtle enough to still look sweet and spring-ready well beyond V-Day. According to shoppers, this shape and size is, “perfect for travel or everyday, fitting your essentials and looking stylish doing it.” And per someone who deemed this a “beautiful accessory,” the Mini Jamie bag is “perfect…for a night out when you don't want to fuss with a lot of weight and you need to compliment your outfit perfectly.”

Gallery Tote In Signature Canvas With Heart Print

Another Valentine’s Day-favorite is this heart-covered version of the brand’s shopper-loved Gallery Tote. I’m obsessed with how the small hearts pop against the dark brown canvas and, of course, with the spacious design. “This is by far my favorite Coach purse,” said one shopper, who explained that they “love” everything from its comfortable shoulder design to its roomy interior. “The quality of [this] Coach bag is phenomenal,” gushed another person, describing the tote as, “durable, lightweight, and very luxurious.” “[It’s] classy, sleek, sexy, professional, and very comfortable on my arm,” raved someone else.
