Check Out This Crazy Throwback WWE Company Christmas Card

When you work for WWE there are a certain amount of perks. Not only do you get a first-hand look at the pro wrestling business, but you are also able to receive major perks like apparently custom employee-only company Christmas cards.

WWE’s warehouse recently showed off an old company Christmas card and as you can see, they went all-out with the design.

Of course, the photo manipulation isn’t perfect in this case because this card was made in the days before Photoshop was readily available to the extent it is now.

Check out the photo below to enjoy Brock Lesnar, Booker T, Triple H, The Undertaker, Big Show, Trish Status, and Kane as cherubs. It’s a visual that you will either hate or love, but it’s likely to be burned into your brain either way.
