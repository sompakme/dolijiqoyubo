Dusty Rhodes Classic Matches Announced for 1/9 WWE NXT Episode

WWE NXT has announced two exciting Dusty Rhodes Classic matches for the January 9, 2024 episode.

The first-round matchups for the Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic tournament promise to deliver thrilling action. Below is the updated lineup for this episode, which is set to air on Tuesday night on the USA Network.

WWE NXT (1/9) Lineup:

  • NXT Tag Team Championship Match: The Family vs. OTM
  • Men’s Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic Match: Bron Breakker & Baron Corbin vs. Gallus
  • Men’s Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic Match: Nathan Frazer & Axiom vs. Hank Walker & Tank Ledger
  • Gigi Dolin vs. Cora Jade
  • Nikkita Lyons vs. Blair Davenport

These matches are sure to provide NXT fans with intense competition and exciting moments as the Dusty Rhodes Classic tournament kicks off. Be sure to tune in to catch all the action and see which teams advance in the tournament.
