See Kendall Jenner Pair a Chartreuse Dress with Leather Gloves in Dubai

Kendall Jenner added some edgy accessories to her latest party look.

The model is visiting Dubai for the grand opening of the ultra-luxury hotel Atlantis The Royal, and she began the weekend by attending a Sake Ceremony hosted by Nobu Matsuhisa and Meir Teper marking the grand re-opening of Nobu Dubai last night. For the jam-packed night, which also included an afterparty for 818 Tequila, the style star paired a bright green fitted gown from Victoria Beckham's spring 2023 collection with sleek leather gloves and boots.

The midi-length dress featured fluttered sleeves and a corseted waist, and Jenner juxtaposed the romantic silhouette with black opera-length gloves and matching knee-length, pointed-toe boots by The Row. She added a pair of large gold earrings, and kept her glam simple and elegant with a nude lip and her hair in a ponytail.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

The style star also posed for a series of gorgeous pics in the look, sharing the behind-the-scenes pics on Instagram.

The 818 founder has been on a style streak this winter, stepping out in enviable chic looks while vacationing in Aspen with younger sister Kylie Jenner and fellow model Hailey Bieber. She also embraced the sexier side of her evening style while attending celeb pal Lori Harvey's 26th birthday party last week.

The star's striking look included a completely sheer one-shoulder gown, and she went sans bra, only wearing visible black underwear. She accessorized the dress with black strappy sandals, silver stud earrings, and a choker adorned with a 3D tropical flower, and her hair was styled in a romantic curly updo.
