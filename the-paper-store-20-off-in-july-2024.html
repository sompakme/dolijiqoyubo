Does The Paper Store offer a birthday coupon?

Yes! When you sign up to The Paper Store newsletter with your email, you'll receive a birthday offer as a treat. You'll also receive the latest news and offers straight to your inbox. 

What payment methods are available at The Paper Store?

At The Paper Store, there are several payment methods available for you to choose from at the checkout stage. They accept payments from major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. They also accept alternative payment methods such as PayPal. 

What is the returns policy at The Paper Store?

At The Paper Store, they have a 45-day returns policy if you’re not 100% satisfied with your purchase. All orders must be returned in their original condition, in their original packaging, and will all tags intact, for you to receive a full refund. For more information regarding individual returns, head over to the Returns FAQs section on The Paper Store website.
